ABOUT US

Our Story

We, Varadom Technologies Private Limited from 2016 has developed into a trustful entity and pioneered the industry by manufacturing optimum quality Automatic vehicle washing System. We offer products range from Automatic Bike Wash System, Automatic Car Washing System, Automatic bus and truck wash system, Automatic train bogie and coach wash system. Semi-Automatic Car and Bike Washing Machine, Smart ETP Plant For Car Washing System, Vacuum Cleaner and all accessories for vehicle wash industry.

Our products are well known in market for their high quality, durable, low maintenance, Easy to service and operate. We have established a sound infrastructure with state-of-the-art facility. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified company. We do have state of the art machinery for fabrication and manufacturing.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To be a global leader in automated cleaning solutions, driving innovation and sustainability in the vehicle and industrial cleaning industries. By FY 2025, we aim to become the most aspirational Indian clean tech brand, consistently winning by:

  • Delivering superior financial returns
  • Driving sustainable cleaning solutions
  • Exceeding customer expectations
  • Creating a highly engaged workforce

Mission

Our mission is to design and manufacture advanced, eco-conscious cleaning systems that meet the diverse needs of our customers. We innovate cleaning solutions with passion to enhance the quality of life, constantly pushing the boundaries of technology, where our innovations are recognized with patents.

We are committed to delivering innovative, reliable, and high-performance solutions, backed by exceptional customer service and continuous improvement.
