Our Services

Customer Care

Our dedicated Customer Care team supports you at every stage of your cleaning system's lifecycle. From technical assistance to spare parts, we ensure your machines keep running smoothly—day in, day out. Your uptime is our priority.

Installation & Commissioning

Our expert engineers ensure the seamless installation and commissioning of your vehicle wash systems. From site preparation to final testing, we handle the entire process so your system is operational from day one.

  • Professional site assessment and preparation guidance
  • Complete assembly and installation of car, bike, bus, and coach wash systems
  • Final commissioning and operational testing
  • Hands-on training for staff on machine operations and maintenance
  • Comprehensive guidance on safety protocols and optimal use

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

Our AMC services are designed to keep your machines running smoothly throughout the year. Regular inspections, preventive maintenance, and timely repairs ensure your system stays in peak condition, reducing the risk of downtime.

  • Preventive maintenance schedules tailored to your equipment
  • Regular system inspections and troubleshooting
  • Replacement of worn-out parts
  • Priority service for repairs and technical assistance
  • Cost-effective solutions for long-term machine reliability

Customization Services

Every business is unique, and so are its washing needs. At Varadom, we offer customization services to tailor our systems to your specific requirements. Whether you need modifications for space constraints, water recycling, or unique wash cycles, we deliver solutions that work for you.

  • Customized machine designs for specific vehicle types or wash processes
  • Tailored features such as water recycling, high-powered drying, and specialized wash modes
  • Bespoke layouts to fit your facility's dimensions and requirements
  • Personalized consultation to develop a wash system that perfectly fits your operational needs

Technical Support & On-Site Repair

We understand that your operations depend on the seamless performance of our systems. That’s why we offer 24/7 technical support to address any issues promptly and ensure minimal disruption to your business.

  • 24/7 Availability: Our technical support team is available round the clock to assist you with any machine-related issues.
  • On-Call Support: Get immediate assistance from our experts via phone whenever you need help.
  • Video Call Assistance: For more complex issues, we offer video call support to guide your team through troubleshooting and repairs in real time.
  • On-Site Repair: If the issue cannot be resolved remotely, our expert engineers will visit your site to provide efficient and timely repairs.
With our 24/7 technical support, whether it's a minor issue or a major breakdown, we’re just a call or video away to get you back on track.

On-Site Training & Consultancy

We believe in empowering our clients with the knowledge to efficiently operate and maintain their systems. Our on-site training programs and consultancy services ensure your team is well-equipped to handle your equipment.

  • Comprehensive on-site training programs for operators and maintenance teams
  • Hands-on workshops on machine operation, safety procedures, and troubleshooting
  • Expert consultancy to optimize the performance of your wash systems
  • Best practices for energy and water efficiency

Machine Relocation Services

If your business requires relocating an existing wash system, our team is here to help. We offer full dismantling, and reinstallation services to ensure your equipment is moved safely and efficiently.

  • Safe dismantling of equipment at the current site
  • Transportation of machines to the new location
  • Full reinstallation and recalibration of the system
  • Post-installation testing and quality checks
  • Minimization of operational downtime during the relocation

Remote Monitoring & Diagnostics

Our smart technology integration allows for remote monitoring of your equipment, ensuring any issues can be detected early. Real-time diagnostics help keep your system running optimally, and any maintenance needs are flagged before they become problems.

  • Remote monitoring systems for real-time performance tracking
  • Diagnostic tools for early detection of potential issues
  • Automated alerts for preventive maintenance
  • Cloud-based data storage for performance reports
  • Expert remote support to resolve issues quickly

Water Recycling Systems Maintenance

Our water recycling solutions help you save water while maintaining high performance in your wash operations. We offer specialized services to maintain and upgrade your water recycling systems, ensuring maximum efficiency.

  • Regular maintenance of water recycling and filtration systems
  • Installation of water-efficient components to minimize wastage
  • Upgrading existing systems with eco-friendly solutions
  • Preventive care to avoid system clogs or downtime
  • Consultation on sustainable water use strategies

Consultancy on Setting up New Wash Centers

For businesses looking to establish new wash centers, our consultancy services provide expert guidance throughout the planning and setup process. From selecting the right equipment to operational strategies, we help you get started with confidence.

  • Site assessment and feasibility studies
  • Equipment recommendations based on your business needs
  • Design and layout consultancy for optimal workflow
  • Cost analysis and ROI projections
  • Guidance on legal and environmental compliance
